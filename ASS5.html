Gopalpur-On-Sea


The City That Whispers Wild Waves Of The Sea Far from the bustling cities, lies a sleepy little port town by the Bay of Bengal. A calm, casual yet sophisticated atmosphere heralds you as you stroll by the pristrine beaches and soak in what was once one of the most happening beach towns in the world in the early 1900s. Twilight ocean views by the lighthouse, the sound of waves crashing and the museum-styled MAYFAIR Palm Beach Resort at Gopalpur on Sea are sure to captivate you time and again.
